Our History

It was a dark and stormy night in mid September 2013 when the original Boomerangs stumbled upon something that could only be described as a gift from above. As Juan Jimenez ran towards what would be the greatest club in the Universe, he had no idea of the greatness that was to come.

“Yo, look what I found guys!” He fumbled through the bushes and pulled out what could only be described as a half triangle from the gods.

The founders dusted off the brown, bent object. “What should we call it…” asked Matt, the soon to be Head Boomer.

“It’s a fucking boomerang you fucking idiot.”

And so begins the story of the Texas Boomerangs.
